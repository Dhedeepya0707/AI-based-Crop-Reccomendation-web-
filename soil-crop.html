Soil Health Improvement Tips

Soil Aeration

Regularly aerate your soil to improve oxygen flow to plant roots and enhance water absorption.

Organic Matter

Add compost or well-rotted manure to improve soil structure and nutrient content.

Crop Rotation

Practice crop rotation to prevent nutrient depletion and reduce pest problems.

Mulching

Use organic mulch to conserve moisture, suppress weeds, and regulate soil temperature.

Cover Crops

Plant cover crops like legumes to fix nitrogen and improve soil fertility.

Water Management

Implement proper irrigation techniques to prevent waterlogging and soil erosion.
