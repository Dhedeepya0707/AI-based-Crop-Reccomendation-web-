Analysis Results

Leaf Blight

Confidence: 92%

About

Fungal disease affecting leaves, stems and fruits

Symptoms

Brown spots with yellow halos on leaves

Conditions

Thrives in warm, humid weather

Affected Crops

Tomato, Potato, Rice, Corn

Recommended Treatment

Organic Solutions

  • Neem oil spray every 7-10 days
  • Baking soda solution (1 tbsp per gallon of water)
  • Copper-based fungicides

Cultural Practices

  • Remove and destroy infected plant parts
  • Improve air circulation
  • Avoid overhead watering
  • Practice crop rotation

Common Crop Diseases

Powdery Mildew

White powdery spots on leaves and stems

Treatment: Sulfur sprays, potassium bicarbonate

Root Rot

Wilting, yellowing leaves, dark rotten roots

Treatment: Improve drainage, fungicide drench

Rust

Orange-brown pustules on leaf undersides

Treatment: Remove infected leaves, fungicides

Bacterial Spot

Water-soaked lesions that turn brown

Treatment: Copper-based sprays, resistant varieties

Disease Prevention Tips

Choose Resistant Varieties

Select crop varieties that are resistant to common diseases in your area.

Proper Watering

Water at the base of plants to keep foliage dry and prevent fungal growth.

Good Air Circulation

Space plants appropriately to allow air movement and reduce humidity.

Crop Rotation

Rotate crops annually to prevent disease buildup in soil.

Sanitation

Clean tools and remove plant debris to eliminate disease sources.

Regular Monitoring

Inspect plants regularly for early signs of disease.
