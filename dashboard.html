About Us

Kisan Sathi is your trusted farming companion. We combine the wisdom of farmers with the power of AI to help in better soil management, crop selection, disease detection, weather forecasting, and market insights.

Our goal is to empower every farmer with modern technology, in simple and local languages. We believe that by bridging the gap between traditional farming knowledge and cutting-edge technology, we can help increase agricultural productivity and sustainability.

The Kisan Sathi project started as a collaborative effort between students from different engineering disciplines who shared a common vision of making agriculture more profitable and sustainable for farmers across India.
